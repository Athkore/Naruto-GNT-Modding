## 26E3 - do_nothing_e3

Do nothing.

## 26E4 - do_nothing_e4

Call `SEQ_RegCMD1` and do nothing.

## 26E5 - handle_playback

Handle a frame of playback of a recording.

## 26E6 - do_nothing_e6

Do nothing.

## 26E7 - do_nothing_e7

Do nothing.

## 26E8 - init_recording

Does one of two things based on the third byte of the opcode. If 0x2, frees memory relating to `seq_p->field15_0x18`. Otherwise, initializes a new recording.

## 26E9 - handle_record

Does one of three things based on the last byte of the opcode. If 0x2, logical `and` recording flag bytes with 0xefffffff. If not 0, finish the recording. If 0, handle a frame of recording of a recording and store a value in `cr`:

- -1 if no more room to record
- 0 otherwise
- 1 in weird circumstances I can't reproduce

## 26EA - get_recording_percent

Get the current recording percentage and store it in `gpr19` and `cr`. The recording percentage out of 100 of the recording storage capacity.

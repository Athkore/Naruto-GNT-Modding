# Opcode Group 24

## 2414

Called when jumping to update air physics of the player.

## 241A

Sets flags on a player:

- 24 1A 00 00 = AF
- 24 1A 12 00 = KF
- 24 1A 48 00 = K2F
- 24 1A 2D 00 = RF
- 24 1A 09 00 = NF
- 24 1A 57 00 = N2F
